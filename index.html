Welcome to Paw Trails! Our Dog friendly hiking guide!

Paw Trails is about going outdoors, hiking and enjoying nature with your dogs. We are compiling a bunch of hikes and walks that you can do together with your furry friends! We will try to add pages to this site as much as we can in order for your dogs to enjoy the outdoors with you!
